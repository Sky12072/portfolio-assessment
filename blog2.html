Rest Enough

Oct 25, 2022   –   3min read

Most of us know that we should rest more, but we don't always do it. We may feel like we don't have time for a break, or like we need to keep working in order to get ahead. However, rest is actually essential for our productivity and well-being.

There is some scientific proof that supports the idea that rest improves our ability to think and code. One study found that people who took a break after learning a new task performed better on tests than those who continued working without a break. The researchers believe this is because taking a break allows our brains to consolidate the information we've learned, making it easier to recall later.

When we rest, our bodies recover from the stress of the day. This helps us to be more alert and focused when we return to work. Additionally, resting allows our brains to process information more effectively. In other words, we're able to think more clearly after a good night's sleep or a relaxing weekend. All of this translates into better coding!

As a web developer, it's important to make sure you rest enough. Working hard is important, but so is giving your body the time it needs to recover. Make sure you get enough sleep and take breaks throughout the day to stay refreshed. Your work will thank you for it!
